Teresa Tarmey

Super Facialist

Recommended by Vogue

With over 26 years' experience, Vogue’s “super-facialist” and Dior's global skin expert from 2021 to 2023, Teresa Tarmey is widely known for her results-driven, clinically-backed treatments and less-is-more philosophy. Her innovative approach to skincare has achieved cult-like status, attracting a wealth of industry insiders and A-list clients alike.

Based between Notting Hill and at The Cadogan Hotel in Belgravia, her clinics offer a variety of cutting-edge treatments, which Teresa and her team tailor-make to target your individual skin needs. Using her unparalleled knowledge, and the highest quality ingredients, Teresa has also created a range of highly effective skincare products designed to bring the unique experience of her clinics to thousands of homes across the world.
